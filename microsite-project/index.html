Quick Park Facts

  • Founded: 1973.
  • Pinnacle Mountain Summit Elevation: 1,011 ft above sea level.
  • Park Size: 2,351 acres of diverse habitats including wetlands and upland ridges.
  • Trail System: Over 33 miles of trails, including part of the 222-mile Ouachita National Recreation Trail.
    • Cultural Significance: Designated a Trail of Tears National Historic Trail, this area witnessed the forced relocation of Cherokee, Choctaw, Creek, Seminole, and Chickasaw peoples.
